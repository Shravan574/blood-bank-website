GUIDELINES

Who should donate Blood?

  • Any healthy adult can donate blood.
  • Men can donate at 3 month intervals while women can do so after every 6 months.
  • One should be above 45kgs in weight.
  • One should fall within the age group of 18-65 years.
  • One should not be suffering from any serious diseases
  • Pregnant and/or breastfeeding mothers should not be donating.

Benefits of Blood Donation

  • Free health checkup! : A trained staff member checks your pulse, BP, Weight, etc.
  • Reveals any potential diseases: Your blood undergoes a screening process for many diseases.
    • In case you test positive for anything, early detection can help you get better sooner.
  • Boosts both your mental and physical health.
  • Helps create fresh blood in your body.

Before donating, make sure you ..

  • Stay hydrated.
  • Have a balanced meal.
  • Have a good night sleep. Do not overwork.
  • Lay off drinks and smoking.
  • Increase the consumption of Iron rich foods.
  • Avoid fatty foods.

While donating, make sure you..

  • Inform the staff know if you are currently on medication.
  • Relax: Keep a calm mind. You can also bring a book or listen to music while donating.
  • Interact with people around you to keep your mind occupied

After donating, make sure you..

  • Do not get up immediately after the process is over, rest for at least 5-7 minutes.
  • Consume the refreshments given to you. Maintain a balanced diet for the next 24 hours and stay hydrated.
  • Avoid strenuous activities.
  • Lay off drinking and smoking for the next 24 hours (at least)
  • Rest properly.
